About

Hi I'm Dana! I am a graphic and web designer living in Harrisburg, PA. I am originally from Scranton, PA where I got my BFA in Graphic Design from Marywood University. I currently work at Silverback Network as a graphic and web designer. In the past I have worked as a freelance designer and an in-house graphic designer for a promotional advertising business.

Design has been a passion of mine for many years. I always am finding ways to further develop my knowledge by reading articles, tutorials, and blog posts to taking classes on Treehouse to learn new skills.

When I'm not designing I love biking, skiing, running, reading, hand-lettering, listening to music, playing with my cat Jonesy, drinking coffee, spending time on news websites and Reddit, and binging on Netflix.
